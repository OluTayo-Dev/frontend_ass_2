Cat Fancy Event 2023

The 115th cat fancy event is being held at New York City on December 21st.

This is the fanciest cat event of the year starting over 300


differents cats. This event was made to show America how great cats really are.

There will be treats at the walk in, and for the cat owners there will be buffet

in the lounge. We thank you for coming to our event site and hope to see you

on December 21st. Thank you
